What Is Hysteria ?

Fulfilling the intention of including every single category of competitions and rocketing each skill set - be it the rhythms of music, the elegant moves of dancing or the verbal impact of literary events to the skies, Hysteria stands unmatched as the annual inter-collegiate cultural festival of DBCL. This cultural festival provides equally important platforms and opportunities to the newcomers, the ones that seek improvement and the ones that participate merely for the thrill.
